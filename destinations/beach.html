About

Rhossili Bay is the first beach to be awarded Britain’s Best Beach by TripAdvisor’s Travellers’ Choice for the second year running, not to mention the 3rd best beach in Europe and 9th best in the world! Rhossili has also been described as ‘The supermodel of British beaches’ by The Independent and has also won accolades from UK Travel Writers and awards for being the best spot to have a picnic! And The Times nominated Rhossili as ‘The UK's No.1 dog-friendly beach’. The main focus of this 7-day holiday is to:


  • Relaxing on the beach - all neccesities included!

  • Enjoying drinks with the locals

  • Getting to know the local culture and history


If this does not get your mind relaxed - then what could!

Itinerary

DAY 1

  1. A town car will pick you up from home in the morning

  2. We will take you to your B&B

  3. A guide will meet with you and provide you with all of the beach necessities, such as:
    •A beach sun lounger
    •Two large beach towels
    •Tanning lotion, sunscreen and an aftersun spray
    •A card containing an unlimited drink and food tab at local cafes and restaurants
    •An activities pass, containing unlimited enty to local museums and national parks

  4. You will finish your first day with a lovely dinner at a local diner

DAY 2-6

  1. Relax on the beach and enjoy local culture!

  2. The B&B will hold daily evening events, such as pop-quiz, tango night and karaoke each day!

  3. Activities included in the package:
    •Visiting Worm's Head Beach, Llangennith Beach and Fall Bay to relax on the beach and explore the nature
    •A guided boat tour to explore the coast
    •Stand-up Paddling experience with an instructor
    •Kayaking with an instructor or individually
    •Snorkelling
    •Sailing around the coast with a chance to swim with porpoises!

DAY 5

  1. You will enjoy a lovely breakfast and will have a chance to go tanning and swimming once more

  2. A private car will pick you and and take you home at 3 pm
