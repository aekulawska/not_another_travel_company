About

An exciting location if you are a fan of adrenaline rushing through your veins! The Honister Mine is operational and has a lot of things to offer:

  • climbing inside and outside the mine with amazing views

  • moving over the air bridge just over the valley

  • walking over the back of the mountain, where any wrong step could be dangerous

  • On top of all that, having a picnic or spend the night just over the sharp ridges.


If this does not get your blood rushing - then what could!

Itinerary

Day 1

  1. We will meet you at the station

  2. We will set you up on the location

  3. We will walk on the tops of the mountains

  4. And we will finish the day with a picnic at the slings

Day 2

  1. We will climb the Honister mine from the inside

  2. Explore the caves, while hanging in the air

  3. Be attached by the bats

  4. Speed fall from the ceiling of the mine

  5. Sleeping in the caves. Ghost stories

Day 3

  1. We will explore the climbing on the outer side of the mine

  2. Speed ropes between the peaks

  3. Celebration in mid air

  4. Reach the top of the mountain

  5. And almost free fall down the mountain
