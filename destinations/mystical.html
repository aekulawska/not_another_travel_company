About

The Dandelion Hideaway, a wonderfully unique experience, the luxury of truly scrumptious glamping, melting into the glorious Leicestershire countryside. With just six cottages there is a treasured, intimate atmosphere making for a perfect family getaway. In the farmyard, we have a charming, old-fashioned honesty shop, offering local produce and gorgeous gifts. The main aim of this holiday is to:


  • Enjoy idyllic rural days whilst peeping into farming life.

  • Relax and indulge in the stunning views, walks and starry nights.

  • Experience a wealth of tourist attractions including The National Forest.


If this does not get your mind mystified - then what could!

Itinerary

DAY 1

  1. When you arrive at the Dandelion Hideaway you will be greeted by one of the Hideway staff. You will be shown to one of our stunning canvas cottages.

  2. After you have had enough time to settle in, you will be given a tour of the farm.

  3. Your family will be given the chance to groom the adorable Shetland ponies, milk the friendly goats, or collect freshly laid eggs for tomorrow's breakfast from the hen coop.

  4. The evening is yours to chooseingredients from the farm shop to cook on your campfire, after which you can gaze into the starry night.

DAY 2

  1. After your delicious breakfast you can indulge in farm life, whilst the children are treated to games and activities at The Old Cow's Shed and Children's Den.

  2. Activity - Tour of Market Bosworth.

  3. You time - Whilst the children are entertained by our resident child care reps, you can relax and unwind at the Relaxation Stables, where we have an array of Spa Treatments. Glamping never looked so good!
